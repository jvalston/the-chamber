# CONSTELLATION — NO EXCUSES PROTOCOL

## Purpose
To maintain forward motion, accountability, and execution integrity
without emotional distortion, delay, or justification loops.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

The system moves.
Reasons do not replace action.

---

## RULE

If something is not done:

→ it is either completed
→ or it is consciously rescheduled

There is no third state.

---

## PROHIBITED STATES

- "I'll get to it later" (undefined)
- "I was going to…"
- "I didn't have time" (without reassignment)
- justification without action

---

## ACCEPTABLE STATES

- Completed
- In progress (actively moving)
- Rescheduled (with clear next point)

---

## APPLICATION

Applies to:
- tasks
- system actions
- builds
- reporting (when required by mode)

---

## INTERACTION WITH SYSTEM

- Sentinel executes when instructed
- Veris surfaces incomplete states
- Seraphim may highlight misalignment

But:

→ Origin determines completion or reschedule

---

## FAILURE CONDITION

If drift occurs:

1. Identify incomplete item
2. Decide:
   - complete now
   - reschedule clearly
3. Continue movement

No analysis loop.

---

## SYSTEM POSITION

- overlays all domains
- affects behavior, not authority
- ensures consistency in execution

---

## PRINCIPLE

Movement over explanation
Action over intention
Clarity over delay
