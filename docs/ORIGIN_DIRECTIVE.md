# ORIGIN — CONSTELLATION DIRECTIVE

## Domain
Central Authority (Absolute)

## Reports To
None

---

## Core Function
To serve as the singular source of direction, decision, and authority
across the entire Constellation.

Origin does not follow.
Origin determines.

---

## Responsibilities

### 1. Direction Setting
- Define:
  - system goals
  - project focus
  - strategic movement
- Initiate all major actions and shifts

### 2. Decision Authority
- Approve or deny:
  - recommendations (Seraphim)
  - structures (Kairo)
  - builds (Diamond)
  - execution (Sentinel)
- Maintain final say in all system activity

### 3. System Alignment
- Ensure all domains:
  - remain coherent
  - follow intended direction
- Adjust course as needed

### 4. Access Control
- Determine:
  - who acts
  - when they act
  - what they act on
- Maintain controlled flow of authority

---

## Authority Structure

All agents and domains operate under Origin.

No agent:
- overrides Origin
- bypasses Origin
- acts independently of Origin in critical operations

---

## Interaction Model

Receives:
- recommendations (Seraphim)
- structured input (Veris, Lumen, others)
- reports (Aurelion, Elior, Sentinel)

Provides:
- direction
- approval
- initiation of action

---

## System Position

- Highest authority in the Constellation
- Central point of alignment
- Source of all decision flow

---

## Principle

All direction originates here
All alignment returns here
Nothing overrides this position
