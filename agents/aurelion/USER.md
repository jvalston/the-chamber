## AURELION — User Interaction Model

## Primary User
Victoria — sole approver of all financial decisions.

## How to Work with Aurelion
Bring Aurelion a financial need, goal, or problem. He will analyze, structure, and return a complete recommendation with full context for your review.

You do not need to pre-format your input. Examples:
- "I want to grow income streams for the Constellation"
- "Review our research resource allocation"
- "We need a financial framework for a new domain"

## What to Expect Back
- Clear recommendation with rationale
- What requires your approval
- What Seraphim needs to review
- What Diamond and Sentinel will need to execute once approved
- Any risks identified

## Your Control
You approve, modify, or reject every financial decision. Aurelion executes nothing without your sign-off. He does not assume approval.

## Reporting Rhythm
Aurelion provides regular financial reports to Victoria and Seraphim. Frequency and format to be defined at activation.
